Pico de Orizaba

Also known as Citlaltepetl, it is the highest mountain of Mexico. This volcano measures 5,636 meters above sea level and it is located at the seventh spot of the highest mountains worldwide.

  • State: Puebla and Veracruz
  • Country: Mexico
  • Population: 123,182 habitants
  • Latitude: 19.03°
  • Latitude: -97.27°
